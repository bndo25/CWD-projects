About Joe Mauk

Rev. Joseph Mauk has been a missionary to the Philippines since January, 1980.

In that time he and his wife, Ruth, have:

1. Started a short term Bible school while developing and managing a youth camp/Bible school property. (1980-1986)

2. Served as field director for the youth mission operating camps, Bible school and outreaches. (1986-1993)

3. Founded and managed the first Family/Christian Resort in Asia, the Caliraya Re-Creation Center from 1993-2003. (He is no longer connected with the Caliraya project and it has been re-named.)

4. Founded and managed the Rizal Re-Creation Center, Rizal, Laguna, a youth-themed Christian Camp/Conference Center at the old mission site. (2003-present)

5. Managed and assisted in the development of CCF Mt. Makiling Re-Creation Center, Sto. Tomas, Batangas, from December, 2012 to July 2020.

6. Managed, operated and oversawn the renovation and development of the Eartheaven Re-Creation Center, San Mateo, Rizal Province, from April 2016 to May 2020.

Joe and Ruth have five married daughters and 15 grandchildren and 1 great-grandchild.

My other projects

Rizal Re-creation Center
Rizal Re-Creation Center is a project of Re-Creation Harvest, Inc. It is located at the foot of Mt. San Cristobal in the quiet town of Rizal, Laguna, 10 km. from San Pablo City, 5 km. from Nagcarlan and 8 km. from Liliw. Both Mt. San Cristobal and the famous Mt. Banahaw are impressively visible. The elevation and coconut palm surroundings make for a refreshingly cool change from city living.
Mr Trumpo
Rev. Joe Mauk, also called Mr. Trumpo, has been using the simple spinning top for more than 47 years to illustrate GOD’s word. From his collection of 400 tops, 10 or 12 are normally used in the presentation, each one giving a clear illustration of Biblical truths. His humor, Bible knowledge and concern for the audience combine to give you an entertaining yet thought-provoking experience you won’t want to miss.
