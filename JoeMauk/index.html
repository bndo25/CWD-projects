Rev. Joseph Mauk has been a missionary to the Philippines since January, 1980.

About Me

Rev. Joe Mauk has been using the simple spinning top for more than 35 years to illustrate GOD’s word. From his collection of 200 tops, 10 or 12 are normally used in the presentation. Each one giving a clear illustration of Biblical truths. His humor, bible knowledge and concern for the audience combine to give you an entertaining yet thought-provoking experience you won’t want to miss.

In that time he and his wife, Ruth, have:

1. Started a short term Bible school while developing and managing a youth camp/Bible school property. (1980-1986)

2. Served as field director for the youth mission operating camps, Bible school and outreaches. (1986-1993)

3. Founded and managed the first Family/Christian Resort in Asia, the Caliraya Re-Creation Center from 1993-2003. (He is no longer connected with the Caliraya project and it has been re-named.)

4. Founded and managed the Rizal Re-Creation Center, Rizal, Laguna, a youth-themed Christian Camp/Conference Center at the old mission site. (2003-present)

5. Engaged in a management agreement with CCF to operate and assist in development of their CCF Mt. Makiling Re-Creation Center, Sto. Tomas, Batangas, from December, 2012 to present.

6. Engaged in a management agreement with Wyden King to operate and oversee the renovation and development of the Eartheaven Re-Creation Center, San Mateo, Rizal Province, from April 2016 to present.

Joe and Ruth have five married daughters and 10 grandchildren.

Rizal Re-creation Center
Rizal Re-Creation Center is a project of Re-Creation Harvest, Inc. It is located at the foot of Mt. San Cristobal in the quiet town of Rizal, Laguna, 10 km. from San Pablo City, 5 km. from Nagcarlan and 8 km. from Liliw, you will find the 7 hectare property of the Rizal Re-Creation Center. Both Mt. San Cristobal and the famous Mt. Banahaw are impressively visible. The elevation and coconut palm surroundings make for a refreshingly cool change from city living.
